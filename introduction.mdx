# Welcome to FixTweetBot

FixTweetBot is a Discord bot that automatically fixes social media embeds using online services like FxTwitter. When you share a link from Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, or 30+ other platforms, the bot reposts it with a 'fixed' version that provides better embeds - allowing videos to play directly in Discord, displaying image galleries, and much more.

## How it works

The bot monitors messages for social media links and automatically:

1. Detects compatible URLs from 30+ platforms
2. Generates a 'fixed' link using the appropriate fixer service
3. Posts the fixed link with better embeds
4. Optionally suppresses or deletes the original message

<Note>
You can prevent the bot from fixing specific links by adding `fxignore` anywhere in your message.
</Note>

## Supported fixers

FixTweetBot relies on excellent open-source fixer services:

- **FxTwitter** - Twitter/X posts with better video embeds
- **InstaFix** - Instagram posts and reels
- **fxTikTok** - TikTok videos with direct playback
- **vxreddit** - Reddit posts with embedded media
- **FixThreads** - Threads posts
- **VixBluesky** - Bluesky posts with media
- **EmbedEZ** - Multi-platform support for Snapchat, IFunny, Imgur, Weibo, and imageboards
- **And 15+ more** - See the [full list](/features/supported-platforms#fixer-services)
